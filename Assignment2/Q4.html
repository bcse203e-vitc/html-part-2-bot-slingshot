Destination Review: Bali

Bali is an Indonesian island known for its forested volcanic mountains, iconic rice paddies, beaches, and coral reefs.

Travel Tips for Bali

  • Don't forget sunscreen!
  • Try the local cuisine, especially Nasi Goreng.
  • Stay hydrated in the tropical heat.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
